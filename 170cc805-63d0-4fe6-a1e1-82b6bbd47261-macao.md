# Macao

## How to get to macao

### Step‑by‑Step Route
- **[Hotel to Hong Kong Port](guide://tell-more)**: From SkyCity Marriott, take a short taxi or hotel shuttle to the HZMB Hong Kong Port. It’s very close to the airport, so the ride is quick.
- **[Golden Bus Shuttle](guide://tell-more)**: At the Hong Kong Port, purchase tickets for the HZMB shuttle bus (known as the Golden Bus). Buses run **24 hours a day**, usually every 5–10 minutes during peak hours and 15–30 minutes late at night.
- **[Crossing the Bridge](guide://tell-more)**: The bus crosses the 55‑km Hong Kong–Zhuhai–Macao Bridge. The journey is scenic and takes about 40–50 minutes.
- **[Arrival at Macau Port](guide://tell-more)**: You’ll arrive at the Macau Port terminal. From here, you can take local buses, taxis, or hotel shuttles to your final destination in Macao.

---

### Key Details
- **[Travel time](guide://tell-more)**: About 1–1.5 hours total, depending on wait times and traffic.
- **[Cost](guide://tell-more)**: Around HK$65–HK$75 per adult (children and seniors discounted).
- **[Tickets](guide://tell-more)**: Buy at the Hong Kong Port ticket counters or online via the HZM Bus website.
- **[Immigration](guide://tell-more)**: You’ll need to clear Hong Kong exit immigration and Macau entry immigration at the respective ports, so keep your passport ready.
